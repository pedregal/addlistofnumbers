This site makes it easy to add a list of numbers, which is also called "taking the sum of numbers". It is really simple to use: paste anything into the text box above, and the calculator will pull out any numbers it can find and add the numbers together. Instructions: The numbers can be in any format (one number per line, comma separated, tab separated). There can even be text between numbers and it will work. Example: "Monday: 3 coffees Tuesday: 1 coffees Wednesday: 0 coffees Thursday: 2 coffees" This calculator recognizes decimal numbers (3.14) as well as negative numbers and will treat those accordingly. It will maintain the right number of significant figures (as in if you enter numbers with 5 sig figs such as 3.1415926, the result will also have 5 sig figs). Performance: This calculator should work well with lists of up to 10,000 numbers. It has worked with larger lists of numbers but it may stall your browser, proceed at your own risk. Why this site: Adding up a list of numbers is something we need to do all the time and it's painful to do. The goal of this site is to make your day just a little bit easier.
